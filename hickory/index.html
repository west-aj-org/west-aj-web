Welcome to Hickory House

Welcome to the Hickory House website. On this site you will find information on all the things that are important to the house. There is a section for Events as well as a section with "Information" about hickory house and what we stand for. If you want to see the current house council and some information on them, you can find that on the "About Us" page, and if you wish to look at our roots you can look at the "Ancestor's" page which has information on the founding members of the council and every person who has sat on the council since its creation. If you wish to connect with your fellow Hickory members then you can go to our facebook page which is our acting forum for all issues you wish to discuss. If you have any questions or comments about the website, you may contact the Webmaster who's information can be found in the "Contact Us" page

Current News

  • We are Live

    The Hickory House website is live and we are looking for stories to post here. Please use the Contact Us page to send in a story you wish to be featured here. The most important stories at the time will be posted here for the house to see.

  • Comments?

    Explore the site and see what you like. If you see anything you don't like about the site and have some creative ideas about how to improve the site, the Webmaster is always up to suggestions for users. Please avoid just saying that you don't like something without being able to explain why or how you think it can be improved. Use the Contact Us page to send the Webmaster your thoughts
