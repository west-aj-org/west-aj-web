Information about Hickory House

Hickory House is the House at the Residential College at West Ambler Johnston that is made up of the first three floors on the southern side of the dorm. Our house tree is, of course, the Hickory Tree while our mascot is the Black Bear. The main traits we wish to inhibit is strength and versatility. Our house color is green which represents the strength and beauty of the forest.

Our Symbols

  • Hickory Tree

    The Hickory tree is a hard, stiff, and versatile. There is other wood that is stronger, but no wood has the strength, the toughness, the stiffness, and the versatility that hickory has. Hickory also has a high energy content and thus is very useful for burning, our wood burns the brightest some say.

  • Black Bear

    The black bear is an omnivore mammal who mostly live in forestsed areas. The black bear is a very territorial animal and mark their territory by rubbing themselves on trees around their territory. Thier fur is not as shaggy as that of the brown bear, and is more smooth. Despite the name, black bears actually display a large amount of colors and show a remarkable amount of individuality. Only about 70% of black bears are actually black.
